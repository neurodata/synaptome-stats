2.1 Transformations

Each of the channels has an arbitrary independent linear transformation and thus converting to z-scores or quantiles is required.

Selecting only the f0 features, we transform by adding 1 and taking the log base 10 for each column. We then scale the transformed data by subtracting the mean and dividing by the standard deviation column-wise.

featF01 <- subset(feat, select=seq(1,144,by=6)) + 1
flog <- apply(featF01,2,log10)
fs <- scale(flog, center=TRUE, scale=TRUE)
#pca <- prcomp(fs, center=FALSE,scale=FALSE)
